About US

Children's Environmental Rescue(CER) formerly Teacher's Environmental Rescue(TER) was formed in 2005. We are an authorised NGO where we work with schools and educational institutions to enhance environmental awareness, conservation and empowerment of the society for climate change adaptation.

Vision

A society that addresses social, economic and health issues responsibly for improved quality of life.

Mission

To optimize the potential of school comunities as a catalyst for;

  • Environmental Conservation and equitable sustainable use of natural resources
  • HIV & AIDS, prevention, care and support
  • Moral and Ethical uprightness poverty alleviation
