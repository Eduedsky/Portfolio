What We Offer

Catering

We offer catering services for our clientele and we are passionate about sourcing, cooking and serving the best fresh food to our Clients, their staff and guests. We boast of well-trained chefs who provide fresh experience into the catering space with our vibrant menus. Using our culinary imagination, we will serve you with contemporary and creative delicious dishes

Cakes & Pasteries

Weevets is your one stop supplier of all your quality and tasty baking needs and solutions. We provide freshly baked products ranging from cakes, cookies and pastries for various events such as birthdays, weddings, corporate functions, anniversaries, engagements and basically all events that deserves a cake. We customize the cakes to your specifications, style and flavours of your choice.

Tents & Decorations

We provide beautiful and stylish tents and chairs for all occasions and weather. We have executive tents and chairs to suit any event both indoors to outdoors.

We pride in uniqueness and extra-ordinary Décor for all occasions. Our team creates stunning setups that are true to your preferred theme and objectives. Weevets team walks with you from conception to execution to create perfect, innovative and stylish events with deco.

We have a variety of fabric colors and lighting options and we can formulate any colour scheme as per your theme. We also offer range of deco options such as, ceiling draping, lighting and backdrops.
