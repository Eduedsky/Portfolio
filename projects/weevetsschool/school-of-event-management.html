School of Event Management

Event management is one of the most exciting and lucrative fields today. Event Management is a process of managing a project. An event could be a meeting, a convention, a trade show, a birthday party, a wedding, or a convetion. Event management outline of this course will include; establishing a vision vision for the event, budgeting for the event, establishing timelines, reserving the event site, acquiring required permits, planning for food, arranging for transport, developing a theme, arranging activities for the participants, arranging for equipment and managing risks. This course starts with the very basics of the event planning and of how event planners work. This course consists of comprehensive and easy to follow course units covering from event planning basics to event planning process, development of themes for the events, planninf budgets for the events, selecting event sites, dealing with health and safety and environmental issues, marketing the event, and finally setting up your own event planning business.


The Event Management course is designed to equip you with art of planning indoor and outdoor, large and medium size functions and gatherings. i.e. Press conferences, charity fund raising events, sports, other social and religious festivals, Birthday parties, weddings etc. The course covers; strategic planning, bidgeting, event promotional techniques, marketing, event operations, organizational skills, human resource maagement and all that is required to work as a professional in event planning industry.
