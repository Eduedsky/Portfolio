About Us

Weevets School of Events is dedicated to turning out well-trained professionals in the field of event management and hospitality using modern education techniques and exposing the students to realistic job settings. Hands-on training is stressed, but theory and relevant course work are also used to provide students with the necessary background and experience to become successful


The school seeks to help student develop positive self-images as well as marketable skills.


The quality of our training and exposure qill enable our students to seek out opportunities to start businesses or get employed after completing our program.


The hallmarks of out training are easily identified through insipiring stories from students. THey are affective instructional methods, hands-on training, and material of educational relevance. We deliver this to our stuents in a friendly and caring atmosphere

  4. Excellence and Quality.
  5. Inspiring to Achieve.

Institutional Objectives

  1. To provide an environment that inspires creativity and entrepreneurship.
  2. To offer our students skills that will enhance their employability.
  3. To incoporate an ethical philosophy in practice throughout our programs.
  4. To create and maintain services and facilities that assist trainers, staff and students to achieve personal grouwth and professional competence
Vision

At Weevets School of Evens, our Vision is to be an insitution recognized for inspiring generations to achieve excellence on Hospitality and Events Management fields

Mission

Weevets School of Events mission is to develop and inspire at all levels, persons to become the best in the Hospitality industry. Our programs are designed to help students learn the skills in the culinary and hospitality fields as well as to develop the habits of lifelong learning needed in today's ever-changing job market.
