School of Catering

The catering Training Program is a dynamic program with a combination of classroom instruction and hands-on training with real life opportunities in the market.


Students work in the school's kitchens in a simulated restaurant and food set up situation preparing meals for classmates, staff and guests. The program prepares the students to undertake business opportunities or seek entry-level employment in a wide variety of food servie operations.
